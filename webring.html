In response to the growing popularity of Web Rings on the World Wide Web and the existing popularity of VIM (Vi IMproved), I thought it was time that VIM users and those wanting to use VIM had a Web Ring of their own. Many VIM users already have Web Pages describing tools that they use with, tricks that they know, and reasons why to use VIM, as well as links to other VIM user's web pages that they are aware of. My vision is that this ring will provide the linkage to VIM user's web pages that are not as well known.


To join, follow this link: JOIN

It will include the HTML Fragment with all of the tagged information filled in based on your entries on the JOIN page. You should be able to cut and paste this into your HTML page and have it work (once I get you entered into the actual Ring).
